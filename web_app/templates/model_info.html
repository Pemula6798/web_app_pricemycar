AI Prediction System

PriceMyCar uses **Predictive AI technology** that acts as a digital professional car appraiser. This AI has learned from thousands of real-world used car sales transactions.

Unlike rigid mathematical formulas, our AI recognizes complex depreciation trends—for example, how the value of a luxury vehicle drops much faster than an economy family car as mileage increases.

Key Parameters Evaluated by AI:

  • Brand & Model: Analyzes the market popularity and demand of your specific car model.
  • Year of Production: Evaluates natural annual depreciation based on the car's age.
  • Mileage (Odometer): Measures physical wear and tear from vehicle usage.
  • Fuel & Transmission: Compares market preference for automatic vs. manual transmissions and fuel types.
  • Ownership History: Assesses the impact of past owners (first owner, second owner, etc.).
  • Usage Frequency: Identifies whether the vehicle was rarely driven or heavily used.
  • Physical Condition Score: Integrates the 10-factor physical evaluation (body, interior, engine, tires, etc.).
