The Algorithm

PriceMyCar uses a HistGradientBoosting Regressor, an ensemble learning method that builds trees sequentially, each correcting the errors of the previous one.

Unlike simple linear regression, gradient boosting captures complex, non-linear relationships, such as how depreciation rate of a luxury car differs drastically from an economy car after the 50,000-mile mark.

Key Features Used:

  • Brand & Model (Frequency Encoded)
  • Year of Manufacture / Car Age
  • Mileage (Log Transformed)
  • Fuel Type & Transmission (OHE)
  • Owner History (Ordinal Encoded)
  • km_per_year & age x km interaction
  • Condition Score (10-factor system)
